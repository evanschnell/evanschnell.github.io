Tips and Tricks!

1. If you want to change something on your page, press Fn, then F12 to inspect element.

2. To change the color of the background, type bgcolor next to the body snippet and put what ever color you please.

3. Looking at computer screen can be harmful for your eyes. Take a break every 10 minutes to give your eyes a rest.

4. Coding is challenging, but if you practice over and over, you will eventually get the hang of it.
